Understanding Your Body Type

Every body is unique, and understanding your body type can help you choose clothes that flatter your figure and highlight your best features. Hereâ€™s a guide to dressing for different body types:

1. Hourglass Shape

Highlight your balanced proportions with fitted clothing that emphasizes your waist. Wrap dresses, high-waisted pants, and belted outfits work well.

2. Pear Shape

Draw attention to your upper body with statement tops and A-line skirts. Dark-colored bottoms can help balance your proportions.

3. Apple Shape

Opt for flowy tops and structured jackets to create a balanced silhouette. V-necklines and empire waist dresses are great choices.

4. Rectangle Shape

Create curves with ruffles, peplum tops, and layered outfits. Belts and fitted clothing can help define your waist.

5. Inverted Triangle

Balance your broad shoulders with wide-leg pants and A-line skirts. Avoid overly padded shoulders and opt for V-necklines.

6. Petite Frame

Choose vertical stripes, high-waisted bottoms, and monochromatic outfits to elongate your frame. Avoid oversized clothing.
